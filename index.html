ABOUT US

The journey of Glow With Wisdom commenced during the zenith of the COVID-19 pandemic in 2020, a period characterized by widespread trepidation and uncertainty. I witnessed the tremendous effects the lockdown measures had on education, good health, and avenues for skill acquisition. Life in Nigeria for so many families was hell. This firsthand experience ignited within me an unquenchable determination to safeguard the sanctity of education and good health while ensuring equitable opportunities for skill acquisition. I started down the path of civic engagement because of an innate desire to make real, positive changes and to promote the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) 1, 2, 3, and 4, namely, no poverty, zero hunger, good health, and well-being, and quality education. Consequentially every step I take forward is fueled by my uncompromising conviction that everyone, irrespective of their circumstances, background, gender, race, and religion deserves the opportunity to unlock their latent potential. Glow with Wisdom endeavors to provide training, online and offline tutorials, seminars, workshops, conferences, skill acquisition opportunities, unwavering support, and academic guidance to enable individuals, particularly the younger generation to surmount impediments and manifest their aspirations.

OUR MISSION

Our mission involves enhancing accessibility to quality education and healthcare while creating avenues for skill development, thereby fostering a more inclusive and empowered society.

Enhance Educational Accessibility:

Employ innovative technologies and tailored programs to broaden access to quality education, ensuring inclusivity and bridging learning gaps across diverse demographics, race, religion, gender and socioeconomic backgrounds

Promote Holistic Health Initiatives:

Implement comprehensive health programs that address physical, mental, and social well-being, fostering a culture of preventive care and wellness.

Facilitate Skill Development for Sustainable Livelihoods:

Spearhead skill acquisition programs designed to empower individuals for the evolving job market, with a focus on fostering adaptable skills that contribute to long-term employability and economic stability.
